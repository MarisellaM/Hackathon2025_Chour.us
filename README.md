# 🎤 Chour.us

**Chour.us** is a social hub for concert lovers — connecting fans, local businesses, and events all in one place.  
Built during **HackUTD 2025**, Chour.us helps fans plan their concert experience, find local recommendations, and collaborate on fan projects.

> "Where every concert becomes a community."

---

## 🌟 Features

- 🎶 **Concert Hub** – View upcoming concerts, venues, and schedules.  
- 🏨 **Local Discovery** – Find top hotels, restaurants, and parking near the venue.  
- 🎨 **Fan Projects** – Collaborate on posters, playlists, or creative fan events.  
- 💬 **Social Feed** – Share memories, photos, and chat with other fans.  
- 🏷️ **Business Promotions** – Local businesses can promote exclusive concert deals.  
- 🧠 **AI-Powered Day Planner** – Uses the **Gemini API** to help fans plan their *concert day* — suggesting timelines for travel, meals, parking, and showtime reminders.

## 🧩 How It Works

1. Search for a concert or artist.  
2. Explore a map of nearby hotels, restaurants, and parking.  
3. Get **AI-generated day plans** powered by Gemini to help coordinate your travel, meals, and timing.  
4. Join or start fan projects and meet other attendees.  
5. See local business promotions and deals tied to your concert.
